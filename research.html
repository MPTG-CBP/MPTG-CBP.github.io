Research
Our research area is theoretical and computational molecular biophysics. Our aim is to understand how biomolecules accomplish their function interacting with other biomolecules. We use concepts of physics and chemistry together with computational tools to assess the dynamics of biomolecules at different spatio-temporal scales, to unravel the physico-chemical principles governing biomolecular behavior. Such an approach allows us to address relevant questions in biology with promising applications in medicine and nanotechnology.
