Welcome to the MPTG-CBP site
The group was established in 2017 and ran until 2020 at the University of Los Andes (Bogotá, Colombia) in tandem with the Max Planck Institute for Medical Research (Heidelberg, Germany). The research area of the group is theoretical and computational molecular biophysics. The goal was to decipher the mechanisms by which macro-biomolecules, particularly in the blood, interact with each other to carry out their functions, in response to external stimuli. Computer simulations at different levels of resolution and complexity were carried out to achieve this goal.
