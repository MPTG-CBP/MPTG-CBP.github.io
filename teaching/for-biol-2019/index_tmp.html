Prerequisites for the course [ ]
The course is addressed to students (both graduate and last-semester undergraduate) and researchers, in the areas of natural sciences and engineering, with basic knowledge in:
- Classical mechanics.
- Thermodynamics and statistical mechanics.
- Structural biology.
- Linux and unix-shell commands.
- Programing.
